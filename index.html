The Crystal Game!

You've been given a random target score. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your score. The amount each crystal holds is different each time you play.

You win the game by reaching the exact target score without going over. Once your current surpasses the target score, you'll lose the game. Give it a go!
